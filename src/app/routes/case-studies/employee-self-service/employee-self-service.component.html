Business Situation

As a truly Namibian company employing 5,568 people in various sectors, the Ohlthaver & List Group is rooted in and committed to, Namibia and all her peoples. With annual revenues of over N$5 billion, O&L is relentless in creating a sustainable future for all and passionately embrace our Purpose: “Creating a future, enhancing life” in everything we do while actively pursuing our Vision, which is: ”To be the most progressive and inspiring company”.
