ABOUT

Founded in 2010, Linx/AS Africa is an established IT service provider in the SAP sector. Linx/AS provides services and products to help drive the usability of SAP on multiple end user devices. In addition we support customers with Biometric Identity Management Solutions in order to secure their SAP® information assets.

Linx/AS Africa is a SAP Silver Partner in the SAP® PartnerEdge® Build Program. Our customers are from a wide range of industries and represent some of the most valued and recognised brands in Southern Africa.

Linx/AS provides these services and solutions in three core focus areas:

  • SAP Usability Services with a focus on enhanced user experiences using SAP® Fiori and the Synactive GuiXT® Suite
  • Identity Management Solutions for SAP using the realTime bioLock® Identity Management Suite for SAP®

  • Product Solution Suite consisting of:

    • Linx/POS for SAP® – A comprehensive and affordable Point of Sale Solution Suite.
